# IEEE Review Synthesis Template

## Complete IEEE Review Format

```
============================================
IEEE REVIEW
============================================

MANUSCRIPT: [Title]
REVIEWER: [Anonymous ID]
DATE: [Review Date]

--------------------------------------------
SUMMARY
--------------------------------------------
[2-3 sentences restating paper's purpose and main findings]

--------------------------------------------
MAJOR CONCERNS
--------------------------------------------
1. [Issue with methodology, data, or analysis]
   Explanation: [Detailed description]
   Impact: [How this affects validity/contribution]
   Suggestion: [How to address]

2. [Next major issue]
   ...

[Number each concern clearly]

--------------------------------------------
MINOR CONCERNS  
--------------------------------------------
1. [Clarity, structure, or presentation issue]
   Suggestion: [How to improve]

2. [Next minor issue]
   ...

[Number each concern clearly]

--------------------------------------------
STRENGTHS
--------------------------------------------
- [Notable positive aspect]
- [Well-executed element]
- [Strong contribution]
...

[Acknowledge what paper does well]

--------------------------------------------
QUESTIONS FOR AUTHORS
--------------------------------------------
1. [Specific question requiring clarification]
2. [Question about methodology or results]
3. [Question about scope or implications]
...

[Questions should help authors improve paper]

--------------------------------------------
SUGGESTIONS FOR IMPROVEMENT
--------------------------------------------
1. [Constructive, actionable suggestion]
2. [Reference to add: Author, "Title," Journal, Year - with justification]
3. [Additional analysis or discussion that would strengthen]
...

[Be specific and helpful]

--------------------------------------------
OVERALL ASSESSMENT
--------------------------------------------
[2-3 paragraphs providing holistic evaluation]

Paragraph 1: Summary of contribution and quality
Paragraph 2: Balance of strengths and weaknesses  
Paragraph 3: Suitability for IEEE publication

--------------------------------------------
RECOMMENDATION
--------------------------------------------
[Accept / Minor Revision / Major Revision / Reject]

JUSTIFICATION:
[Detailed explanation of recommendation following decision tree logic]

[If revision: specify what must be addressed and timeline expectations]
[If reject: explain why issues cannot be resolved through revision]

============================================
END OF REVIEW
============================================
```

## Review Synthesis Checklist

Before submitting review:
- [ ] All sections completed
- [ ] Specific, actionable feedback provided
- [ ] Professional, constructive tone throughout
- [ ] No identifying information included
- [ ] Major/minor distinction clear
- [ ] Recommendation justified by review body
- [ ] Questions are genuine and helpful
- [ ] Suggestions are reasonable
- [ ] No excessive reference suggestions
- [ ] Grammar and clarity checked

## Quality Standards

**Professional tone**: Respectful, objective, constructive
**Specific feedback**: Line numbers, examples, clear descriptions  
**Actionable guidance**: Authors know how to address issues
**Fair evaluation**: Balanced view of strengths and weaknesses
**Appropriate recommendation**: Follows decision tree logic
